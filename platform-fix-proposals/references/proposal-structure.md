## 2. Executive Summary

Three paragraphs max:
1. Reference discovery call, state purpose
2. Client situation (teams, scale, challenges)
3. What this document presents

## 4. Business Context

**Current Situation** (bullets): Platform description, team size, current scale, growth target

**Critical Challenges** (bullets): 5 specific challenges using their words

**Desired Outcomes** (bullets): 5 tangible outcomes

## 5. Cost of Waiting

Warning box: "EVERY MONTH WITHOUT ACTION COSTS YOU"

| Cost Factor | Impact |
|-------------|--------|
| Productivity loss | £X-Y/month |
| Delayed delivery | Unquantified |
| Risk factor | Specific risk |
| Bottleneck | Quantified |
| **ANNUAL COST** | **£X-Y+** |

Follow with payback calculation: "Foundation pays for itself in X-Y months"

## 6. Why Platform Fix

**We Delete, We Don't Add**: Philosophy paragraph

**Engineers, Not MBAs**: Credibility paragraph

**Track Record**: Stats box (53 transformations | 40-70% reduction)

**Case Study**: Challenge, approach, 4 results with checkmarks, quote

## 7. Why This Offer

Two reasons maximum:
1. Market/strategic fit
2. Timeline alignment

Close: "This isn't discounted work. It's full-price work at a moment when the fit is perfect."

## 8-11. Engagement Options

### Audit

- Price and duration
- What's included (bullets)
- What's NOT included
- Delivery method
- Outcome statement
- **Upgrade Credit**: 60-day credit toward Foundation

### Foundation ★ RECOMMENDED

- Price and duration
- **Value Stack Table**: Components with £ values
- Savings statement: "£X value for £Y, Z% saving"
- Delivery timeline by week
- **Bonuses Table**: 2-3 extras with values and expiry
- Outcome (dream statement)
- **Guarantee Box**: 3 specific guarantees with terms

### Momentum

- Price and duration
- What's included (Foundation + advisory details)
- Why this option (tied to their scaling needs)
- Outcome statement
